About Us

Description

KEG is an association that creates quality cloths with the KEG logo on it. The only purpose of Project KEG ,is to make sure that everybody wears a Magnificent and Original cloth every day of their life, made in the US, more precisely in California, the cloth is made of cotton, which is a very soft and breathable material, studies have provided facts about the durability of cotton, regardless of it softness cotton are strong, dye absorbent and can stand up against abrasion wear and high temperature. In one word, cotton is comfortable and that's why we decided to us this material, to give the best to our customers. A comfortable cloth, makes life easier.

Logo

As you noticed the KEG logo has three letters, each on of them has it's own profound meaning and reference.


K

The K stand for Khepris, this name may not mean anything to you, but you must know that Khepris is the name of an ancient Egyptian Deity, he is the God of The Rising Sun and he's often represent as a Dung Beetle carrying the Sun.

E

The E stands for an ancient Greek Deity called Eros, who is the primordial God and Personification of Love, he is represented as handsome young man.

G

The third and last letter is the G which stands for one of the greatest Hindu God ever, Ganesha, who is represented as a humanoid Elephant, he became like that after his own father Shiva cut off his head, thinking he was an indruder, and then Shiva replaced his head with that of an Elephant, the wisest animal on earth, he is the God of Wisdom and Luck.

As you can see I was inspired by those three Gods, and I was espetially attracted by the principle they incarnate. I stated earlier that the KEG logo, has a profound meaning, and this is what we wanted that logo to represent, something deep and profound. You may have also noticed that the logo looks like a globe or a circle, and it's indeed a globe, the KEG logo represent The Rising Sun of Wisdom, Spreading Love and Fortune. It is quit strang to realize that I found inspiration in my own names.
